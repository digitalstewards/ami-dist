What is a PIPEDA request?

PIPEDA contains 10 Fair Information Principles, one of which (Principle 4.9) relates to individualsâ€™ right to access personal information data operators (such as telcos) hold about them.

In general, the Act obliges data operators to give individuals access to their personal information on request. Operators have to do this in a complete and timely way, at little or no cost to the requester. (Source: Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada)
